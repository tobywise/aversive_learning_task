When you enter the cave, you will enter through one of four entrances.

Each entrance will take you to a different type of rock. There are two different types of rock.

Your first job is to learn which entrance takes you to which rock. 
*First, you can try entering the cave through different entrances. Try to learn which entrance leads you to which type of rock.

You will be tested after to see whether you have learned which entrance leads to which type of rock.

You will not encounter any snakes during this training period.
